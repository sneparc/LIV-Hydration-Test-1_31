About Us:

LIV Hydration provides discreet, professional concierge medicine in the comfort of your own home or office. Our products range from IV fluids to pain relief to vitamin injections. Appointments may be scheduled as a one-time visit or on a recurring, regular basis. Feel free to contact us with general medical inquiries as well. Although we specialize in rehydration and revitalization, our goal is to work with the patient and meet any and all needs. Our medical professionals are all licensed by the State of New York and we look forward to treating you and helping you feel better quickly.
